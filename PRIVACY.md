Privacy Policy for Shorts Limiter

Last updated: 21.02.2026

This extension collects and stores the following data locally on your device:

- Daily time spent watching YouTube Shorts (stored in chrome.storage.local)
- User settings such as daily time limit and redirect video URL (stored in chrome.storage.local)
- Statistics history of your daily usage (stored in chrome.storage.local)

Data Storage:
- All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No personal information is collected or shared with third parties

Permissions Used:
- storage: To save your settings and usage statistics locally
- alarms: For daily reset functionality
- notifications: To alert you when you reach your daily limit
- tabs: To detect YouTube Shorts pages and perform redirects
- host_permissions (youtube.com): To inject the content script and monitor Shorts usage

Your data remains on your device and is never accessible to us or any third parties.
